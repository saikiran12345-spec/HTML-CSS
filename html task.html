procedure to do chicken fried rice

  • chicken
  • edible oil
  • rice
  • chicken masala
  • water
  1. fry the chicken
  2. add water
  3. add chicken masala
  4. leave it for an hour
  5. garnish with coriander

A big bowl of Chicken Fried Rice never lets you down, because it is tasty, healthy and super-quick to put together! One of the best part about this rice stir-fry is that you can easily customize it by adding your favourite vegetables, which also adds more colour, nutrition and texture to the dish. It is best served with complementary gravy-style Indo-Chinese dishes like Chicken Manchurian and Chilli Chicken or you can just try it with some schezwan sauce - if you like to challenge your taste buds with spiciness every now and then!
